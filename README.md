Tools: Excel (Pivot Charts, Slicers, Pie/Bar Charts), Data Cleaning, Data Visualization

Key Insights Achieved:

Analyzed annual sales performance showing high sales volume in January with strong correlation between order count and revenue generated.

Identified women as the dominant buyer segment contributing 56% of total sales, compared to 44% by men.

Performed region-wise analysis revealing Maharashtra (27%), Uttar Pradesh (25%) and Karnataka (20%) as top-performing states driving majority of revenue.

Order trend breakdown showed 1,027 successful deliveries versus a minimal number of cancellations and returns, indicating high fulfillment success rate.

Customer demographics highlighted Women (28.3%) and Men (21.9%) adults as the primary purchasing group, influencing inventory and promotion targeting.

Platform segmentation analysis revealed Ajio and Amazon contributing highest order share (36% & 23%), helping recommend focused marketplace strategy.

Implemented slicers for Category, Channel, and Month filters enabling stakeholders to interact with live data and make dynamic decisions.

Improved business visibility by creating a unified dashboard showcasing sales performance, customer profiles, fulfillment status, and platform contribution.
